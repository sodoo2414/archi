BARBAROS SAĞDIÇ

Prof. Dr. Arch. BARBAROS SAĞDIÇ was graduated from Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, Faculty of Architecture in 1975. He started lecturing the Project Workshops and Building Design as Assist. Prof. at Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, Faculty of Architecture in 1977. During the same period he has earned several awards and honorable mentions by participating in national and international architectural competitions. BARBAROS SAGDIC who has continued his academic career at the abroad universities, has lectured the Project Workshops and Architectural History disciplines as Prof. Arch. at University of Brighton, University of Oxford, European University of Lefke ,University of Chicago, University of Barcelona, and San Sebastian University. Around the world in different countries. He has put his signature under the project designs and works with especially Kisho Kurukawa, Philip Johnson and Alan Ritchie. He has won many awards in Best High-Rise Building, Best MixedUse Project, Best Dwelling, Most Secure Building, with his projects of Vazo Tower, Dalga Toweri Burgu Tower, Royal Centre and Royal Towers projects in 2013 - 2014 in European Property Awards which is held annually in London, capital of England and is the most prestigious architectural award.
